🔍 What Your IP Address Reveals About You

It's more than just a number.

Your public IP address tells every website you visit:

📍 Your city and region — accurate to within a few miles

🏢 Your ISP name — Comcast, Starlink, Digicel, etc.

🌐 Your country — and whether you're on a VPN or not

⏰ Your browsing patterns — sites can track you across visits

Want to see for yourself? Visit YesMyIP.com — it takes 2 seconds. If you can see your city and ISP, so can every other website.

💡 3 Things You Should Do Right Now

1

Turn on DNS-over-HTTPS

Chrome → Settings → Privacy → Security → "Use secure DNS" → turn it ON. This stops your ISP from seeing which websites you visit. Takes 10 seconds.
2

Check if your passwords have been leaked

Go to haveibeenpwned.com and enter your email. If it shows up in a breach, change that password immediately. Use a different password for every site.
3

Get a VPN (takes 3 minutes)

A VPN hides your IP address, encrypts your traffic, and keeps your ISP from tracking you. It's the single most impactful thing you can do for your online privacy. Our top pick is below.
