Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

YesMyIP.com uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the site to function properly:

  • cookieConsent — Remembers your cookie preference (accept/reject). Stored in localStorage.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand site usage. These cookies are only set if you click "Accept All" on our cookie banner:

  • _ga — Distinguishes unique users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_* — Maintains session state. Expires after 2 years.

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense may set cookies to display relevant ads. These are only active if you accept cookies:

  • Various Google advertising cookies — see Google's ad cookie policy

Affiliate Cookies

When you click an affiliate link to a VPN provider, that provider may set cookies to track the referral. These cookies are set by the VPN provider's website, not by us.

How to Control Cookies

Cookie banner: When you first visit, you can choose "Accept All" or "Essential Only."

Browser settings: You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Opt-out links: Google Analytics Opt-out
