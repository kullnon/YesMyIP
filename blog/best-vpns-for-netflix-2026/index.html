Why Does Netflix Block VPNs?

Netflix licenses content separately for each country. A movie available in the US might not be licensed for the UK. Content owners require Netflix to enforce these geographic restrictions, so Netflix blocks known VPN IP addresses.

How to Watch Netflix with a VPN

Step 1: Sign up for a VPN that works with Netflix (we recommend NordVPN).

Step 2: Download the VPN app on your device.

Step 3: Connect to a server in the country whose Netflix library you want to access.

Step 4: Open Netflix and start streaming. The library will automatically show content for your VPN server's location.
