Want to try a VPN before committing? Most top VPNs don't offer traditional free trials — but they all have money-back guarantees that work even better. Here's how to use them.

The Money-Back Guarantee Trick

Every premium VPN offers a 30-day (or 45-day) money-back guarantee. Sign up, use the full VPN with zero limitations, and cancel before the guarantee period ends for a complete refund. No questions asked. This is better than a free trial because you get the full product — no speed limits, no data caps, no feature restrictions.
