What Is an IP Lookup?

An IP lookup (also called IP geolocation) reveals the approximate physical location and network information associated with any IP address. When you enter an IP above, we query geolocation databases to find the country, city, ISP, timezone, and coordinates linked to that address.

How Accurate Is IP Geolocation?

Country-level: 99%+ accuracy. We can almost always determine which country an IP belongs to.

City-level: 50-80% accuracy. The city shown is an approximation based on ISP data — it could be off by 10-50 miles, especially in rural areas.

Street-level: Not possible. IP addresses do not reveal home addresses, apartment numbers, or precise locations. Only your ISP can link your IP to your physical address, and they require a legal warrant to share that information.
